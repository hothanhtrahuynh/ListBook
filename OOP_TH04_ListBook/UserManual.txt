HUONG DAN SU DUNG
=================
- Neu ban da co tai khoan thi chon DANG NHAP de thuc hien cac thaot ac ke tiep.4
- Neu chua co tao khoan thi ban co the chon TAO TAI KHOAN de co the dang nhap va thuc hien cac thao tac khac.
- Doi voi nguoi dung chua co tai khoan thi chi co the xem sach hien co tren he thong.
- Neu muon mua sach thi buoc quy khach phair co 1 tai khoan de dang nhap.
=================
1.Dang nhap:
Sau khi chon --> Nhap ten tai khoan --> Nhap mat khau
Sau khi dang nhap thanh cong thi quy khach co the thuc hien bang cach chon lenh tuong ung dung truoc ten cac chuc nang nhu:
--------------------
*Doi voi User:
1. Mua sach.
2. Xem danh sach hoa don
3. Cap nhat so luong moi cho hoa don.
4. Xoa hoa don
5. Gui tin nhan (Chi duoc gui tin nhan cho cac User khac)
6. Xam cac muc uu dai.
--------------------
*Doi voi Nha Xuat Ban:
1. Xem danh sach sach.
2. Them sach vao danh sach. 
3. Cap nhat lai gia sach.
4. Xoa sach.
5. Gui tin nhan.(Chi gui duoc tin nhan cho cac NXB khac)
--------------------
*Doi voi Tac Gia:
1. Xem danh sach sach.
2. Them sach vao danh sach. 
3. Cap nhat lai ten sach.
4. Xoa sach.
5. Gui tin nhan.(chi gui duoc tin nhan cho cac Tac Gia khac)
--------------------
*Doi voi Admin
1. Them tai khoan.
2. Them sach.
3. Khoa quyen tac gia.
4. Khoa quyen nha xuat ban.
5. Xem danh sach tai khoan.
6. Xem danh sach sach.
7. Xoa tai khoan.
8. Xoa sach.
9. Gui tin nhan.
	1. Gui dong loat cho USER.
	2. Gui dong loat cho Tac Gia.
	3. Gui dong loat cho NXB.
	4. Gui dong loat cho tat ca tai khoan.
	5. Gui cho mot tai khoan cu the
10. Thong ke.
--------------------
